1. About Iéseg

Founded in Lille in 1964, and being a member of the Université Catholique de Lille, the Conférence des Écoles and the FESIC, IÉSEG School of Management is one of the best management schools in the world and is part of the very select circle of Schools that have been awarded the triple accreditation EQUIS, AACSB and AMBA. With a mission to empower changemakers for a better society, the School offers its 7,000 students, spread over its two campuses in Lille and Paris-La Défense, a wide variety of post-bac, post-graduate, executive and tailor-made programs for companies.
